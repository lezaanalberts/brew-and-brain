Our Story

Brew&Brain started out as a small business idea between two friends who wanted to make an extra living. Now, 10 years later we're supporting young minds by giving them a space to study without pressure and a space where they can drink as much coffee and hang out together with friends as they want. As students ourselves, we've always wanted a place to study and stay motivated to chase our dreams, but also a place where we could socialise and make new friends. And so began Brew&Brain!

We now make our own special blend of coffee grounds, harvested by our partners in Brazil and Vietnam, ensuring the best possible quality for coffee enthusiasts at all our branches. We started off small as just one branch in Cape Town. Now we have branches all over South Africa, spanning from Potchefstroom to Gqeberha to Benoni.

Our vision is to always keep an open mind and to always welcome change. You never know what good things can come from them. We know that change also brings challenges, but that means we'll grow and learn in the process as well.
